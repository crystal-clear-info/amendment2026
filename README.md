# CRYSTAL CLEAR: a resource for trans people & allies to understand the impact of Trans Bill 2026

In the wake of the Trans Amendment Bill 2026 going through the Parliament, getting passed, and becoming an act, the trans community in India has faced increased pressures. Increased stress, issues from medical professionals refusing to serve them, and fears of how we'll go on with our lives now. The amendment's text itself is vague, and there are questions and misinformation abound. What should we do about our documents? What should we know for our friends? What does this mean for our futures?

Crystal Clear was started after seeing how lack of clarity about *what comes next* has caused panic among trans folks and allies. This project will be a website which presents frequently asked questions about the situation in an accessible way. 

Our team consists of volunteers: researchers, archivists, writers, and designers. If you are interested in volunteering or have any questions, please email crystal.clear.trans.info@gmail.com
